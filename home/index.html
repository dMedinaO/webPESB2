pesb2 group

A multidisciplinary group

We are a group with experts in research related to biological systems, applying mathematical modeling techniques, bioinformatics, machine learning, data mining and experimental techniques. The use of different approaches allows us to be a multidisciplinary group with great capacities in different areas of research.

Mathematical Modeling

Mathematical modeling is applied to the study of complex systems, in order to understand the mechanisms behind them.

Proteing Engineering

Protein engineering focuses on the study of proteins and the understanding of their properties for industrial or commercial applications.

Data Mining and ML

We use ML and data mining for manipulation of large volumes of data and that allows predicting behaviors, optimizing experimental processes.

Bioinformatics

We know and apply different bioinformatics techniques at genomic studies, simulations of proteins interactions and computational biology.
