PESB2 Group.

Protein engineering, simulations, bioengineering and bioinformatics (PESB2) is an interdisciplinary group with principal research in protein engineering applied different techniques and methodologies for studying mutations process, to design synthetic peptides, and, family evaluation of proteins. Recently, PESB2 has shown strong competitivity in Machine Learning and mathematical modeling areas, applied to study mutations in proteins and to model biological complex systems.

PESB2 Group

Different areas of research are of interest to PESB2 group. In particular Mathematical Modeling, Machine Learning, Bioinformatics and, protein engineering are the main focus. Making different projects and studies applying these topics research. The study of Glucose-Insulin control system, predictive models applied to analyze the effect of mutations in proteins, bioinformatics applications for simulations structures of protein, and experimental techniques to validate the results of our model it makes to PESB2 group a multidisciplinary group.

Mathematical Modeling

Design and implement mathematical modeling for studying complex biological systems. With the main focus in applications to personalized medicine and optimization process.

Bioinformatics

Different bioinformatics techniques are applied to analyze structure proteins, effects of mutations, and phylogenetic information for the study of families.

Protein Engineering

The study of physicochemical properties, effects of mutations, and evaluation of interaction protein complex systems are studied in protein engineering.

Machine Learning

Different techniques are applied to develop predictive models in protein engineering. Besides, it applies ML to pattern recognition in the study of complex systems.

Projects and Tools

Mathematical Modeling Projects

Mathematical modeling of Glucose-Insulin control system for the determination of parameters with relevance on the health status of patients and to make personalized medicine

Pattern Recognition Projects

Pattern recognition in behaviors of regulation glucose by the system GI. Clustering in protein sequence based on the digitization of physicochemical properties and graph properties.

Machine Learning Projects

Predictive models to study the effect of single point mutations in proteins. Machine Learning algorithm to evaluate variants of proteins, and Classification models applied to clinical study.

Bioinformatics Applications

Analysis of sequences in genomes for recognizing functions or families. Structural modeling, docking, and simulations antigen-antibody interactions. Genomic analysis of families for getting statistical tendencies.

Protein Engineering Projects

Study in-silico and in-vivo of mutations in proteins, with a focus in peptide design, applied to the treatment of VIH, evaluating different comportment in structure and reactions between protein and agent effector.

Molecular Biology Projects

Molecular biology techniques applied to study gluten and design products without this substance. To comprehend the interactions of proteins and effective agents in VIH, facilitating the design of peptides.

Development software Projects

Development of different software, applications, and tools, allowing the use the data mining and machine learning techniques, showing our predictive models, and applications of our mathematical modelings.

Projects Funding

The projects are mainly funding by the Centre for Biotechnology and Bioengineering CeBiB and Fondecyt projects. All our members have a Conicyt fellowship or another form to financing their investigation.

Collaboration

Actually, the members of the group have collaborations with different institutions, for example, Maqui New Life, Universidad de Magallanes, and the University of Cambridge, among others, in different projects and different research.
