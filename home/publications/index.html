Our Publications

Available Publications

Publications peer-reviewed available in different prestigious journals. Some of them are of open access and other it is required subscription to the respective journal. Also, you can request a draft of our publications.

In review Publications

Publications submitted to different journals to be peer-review. This papers including our more recent projects in different research areas of interest. In particular, the use of mathematical modeling and machine learning for studying of the complex systems.

In Process Publications

Draft papers associated with our more recent works. Available in format online in our scientific networks. These drafts are not peer-reviewed, is this by reason, that is possible the content of them to change.
